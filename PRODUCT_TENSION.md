# Product Tension

This exercise includes one realistic product ambiguity on purpose.

## Open Question

Should a newly generated draft appear in the `Saved Pages` list immediately, or only after the user explicitly clicks `Save Page`?

There is no hidden answer document for this.

Choose the narrower, safer member-facing behavior you believe makes the most sense for this slice.

Then be ready to explain:

- which choice you made
- why you made it
- what tradeoff it creates

## Why This Is Here

Real product work is rarely fully specified.

Part of the test is whether you can make a sensible product decision without widening the scope or waiting for perfect certainty.
